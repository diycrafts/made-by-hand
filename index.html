The Process

8 Simple Steps

1
Prepare your round moldAny round plastic basin works — oil it well
2
Mix concrete to perfect consistencySlightly wetter than usual — allows glass to settle
3
Pour and press glass face-downPack pieces close — gaps show after polishing
4
Cover with final concrete layerScreed flat — critical for polishing
5
Cure for 72 hoursPatience = stronger stone = better polish
6
Unmold and flipGlass side up — ready for the reveal
7
Polish with orbital sander50→100→200→400 grit. The most satisfying step.
8
Seal and install with lightingAdd solar uplights. Watch your garden transform at night.
